About Us

At Chess Titans Academy, we provide an interactive platform for chess enthusiasts of all levels. Our dedicated instructors guide you through the strategies and intricacies of the game, ensuring a rewarding learning experience.

Mr. M.J STEVE

Welcome to Chess Titans Academy, where passion for chess meets expert guidance!

At Chess Titans Academy, we are dedicated to nurturing chess enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. With over 5 years of teaching experience, our founder has a proven track record of success in the chess community. Notably, they secured the 8th rank in the prestigious Madras University Chess Tournament 2023 and were crowned the Tamil Nadu State Champion in 2018.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive learning environment that combines strategic instruction with interactive play. We believe that chess is not just a game but a powerful tool for developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Join us to embark on your chess journey, where you will learn from a champion and become a part of our vibrant chess community!
